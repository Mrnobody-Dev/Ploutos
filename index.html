About PLT

Tokennomics

Ploutos (PLT) has a total supply of 2 million tokens, ensuring scarcity and value. Transaction fees are flexible, ranging from 2% to 5%, with fees directed to a treasury wallet for further development and community-driven initiatives.

How Ploutos works

When users send or trade PLT, a small transaction fee (2-5%) is applied. This fee is collected in a treasury wallet to fund growth, partnerships, and rewards. Holders benefit from a stable and sustainable ecosystem.

Utility & Use cases

Ploutos offers more than just a store of value. Its utility extends to staking, decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, and more. The token's adjustable transaction fees help maintain its long-term sustainability, making it a reliable asset in the crypto ecosystem.

How to buy Ploutos (PLT)

White paper

Ploutos White Paper

1. Introduction

Ploutos is a next-generation, community-driven cryptocurrency project that combines transparent tokenomics, strong community incentives, and an innovative educational platform to empower its users. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where participants are rewarded not only for their investment but also for their commitment to learning and contributing to the decentralized finance (DeFi) revolution.

By integrating a controlled token supply with exclusive benefits and comprehensive educational resources, Ploutos aims to bridge the gap between traditional finance, blockchain technology, and the global community. We believe that informed participation is key to driving adoption, and our platform is designed to offer a clear path toward financial empowerment and blockchain literacy.

2. Problem Statement

The current crypto landscape faces several challenges:

  • Opaque Tokenomics: Many projects lack transparency in how tokens are distributed and minted, leading to uncertainty and potential imbalances in supply.
  • Inadequate Community Engagement: Early adopters and active community members often do not receive sufficient rewards or recognition, which can hinder long-term commitment and growth.
  • Limited Access to Quality Education: There is a significant shortfall in accessible, reliable educational resources that help newcomers understand blockchain technology, crypto investments, and decentralized finance.

Ploutos addresses these challenges by providing a platform that emphasizes transparency, rewards community loyalty, and integrates education directly into its ecosystem.

3. Our Solution

Ploutos is built on three core pillars:

3.1 Transparent and Controlled Tokenomics

Minting Process: Our smart contract includes a mint function that is exclusively controlled by the project’s owner. Each execution mints around, 25,000/100,000 the community can make a choice between those numbers, ensuring that everything is transparent.

Hard Cap Enforcement: A predefined maximum supply (MAX_SUPPLY) is set to maintain scarcity, which is critical for long-term value preservation and market stability.

Example Calculation: With 18 decimals (standard for ERC20 tokens)

3.2 Community-First Incentives

Rewarding Early Supporters: From the start, early adopters and active participants receive exclusive benefits. These include VIP roles and other community-specific rewards that recognize and incentivize long-term engagement.

3.3 Empowering Education

Dedicated Learning Platform: Ploutos will launch a separate, fully integrated website offering a range of blockchain and cryptocurrency courses. These courses are designed to be accessible to both newcomers and seasoned investors.

Course Model:

  • Cost: Each course is priced at $100 for example.
  • Incentive: Participants who successfully complete their course will earn back $50.

Long-Term Impact: By equipping our community with critical knowledge, we foster informed decision-making and empower users to navigate the complexities of decentralized finance with confidence.
